Our Story

King Pin Donuts is a family owned and operated business open since the 1930's. We use fine ingredients and traditional methods to produce exceptional donuts, cakes, muffins, pastries and cookies. We have been a pioneer in the baking industry being the first to offer trans-fat free products in the US.

While we continually strive for innovation at King Pin Donuts, we're still making our creations the way Dari (the new owner since 1980) intended to feed his young kids: high quality and fresh daily. We are proud and honored provide to our customers.
